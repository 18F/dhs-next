Benefits

Full time employees with the U.S. Digital Service are elgible for health insurance, dental and vision, and the Thrift Savings plan, the government's version of a 401k that offers up to five percent matching.

Tours of duty

We developed tours of duty to provide tech talent with the opportunity to serve their country for a time limited term. Most people sign on for one year, but there are some opportunities for shorter or longer term engagements.

Dress code

Don’t worry—most days you can dress like you’re at any other startup. Every now and then we dress up for important meetings, so it’s good to have at least one formal outfit in your closet.

Compensation

Your specific background, including current salary and years of experience, will ultimately determine your pay. Digital Service salaries are set in accordance with the General Schedule Pay Scale, which is capped at $160,300.

Relocation

All of our roles are located in Washington, D.C. We’re working shoulder to shoulder with leaders across the federal government, so it’s important for us to be in the same room.

Unfortunately, we’re unable to provide relocation assistance.

Background check

All employees are required to pass a background check, and most agencies also require that you pass a drug test.
