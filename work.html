Vets.gov

With more than 1000 websites and 956 phone numbers, the VA customer experience is fragmented, frustrating, and confusing. Working with leaders at the VA, we are building Vets.gov, a simple, easy to use site that provides Veterans with useful information information that is clearly written and presented in one place. Vets.gov launched in November 2015 in beta, and is being put in front of Veterans as early as possible so we can see, as we build, what is working for them and what is not. As the site progresses, Veterans will be able to find and get more of what they need in one place.

Online Immigration

We're partnering with U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services to digitize a formerly paper-based process to review over seven million immigration applications each year. Over a quarter of our country's immigration system is now digital, including the green card application (I-90), which has earned a 93% satisfaction rate from users. By working with USCIS to improve software development practices, system architecture, and design processes, we're giving new Americans a more efficient way to apply for citizenship.

College Scorecard

For students, selecting a college may be the single most important investment of their lives, but finding clear, reliable data on critical questions is a challenge. We launched College Scorecard to empower students with the most comprehensive, reliable data ever published on employment outcomes and success in repaying student loans. 1.4+ million students have benefited from these data, and the College Scorecard has been accessed over 9.4 million times in less than 6 months.
