Available positions

We're currently looking to fill multiple positions in the following roles:

Cloud Engineer

You'll be responsible for engineering a suite of integrated, scalable, reliable, and secure cloud services for the entire enterprise. You'll also explore Platform as a Service solutions to enable teams to develop, run, and manage applications rapidly and easily.

Senior Agile Expert

You'll be responsible for reengineering our institutional processes for governing and delivering technology from an old tradition of "waterfall" to a new tradition of Lean and Agile. You'll have to be effective at convincing individuals and groups the value of such change.

Technology Analyst

You'll be responsible for discovering, piloting, and scaling the use of cutting-edge technologies to meet our critical mission needs. You'll effectively serve as the bridge between those on the frontline of delivering our mission and those companies operating at the bleeding edge of technology.

Join us

What could being part of the DHS mission mean to you?

As a member of DHS Next, you'll serve on a tour of duty up to four years. You'll partner with talented civil servants to spread lean startup, human centered design, data science, and agile practices throughout the Department of Homeland Security.

Come fundamentally transform how 240,000 people leverage digital technologies in keeping our homeland — from commerce to transportation to people's lives — secure against the millions of daily threats we face.

It'll be one of the most challenging, yet enriching experiences of your professional life.

To apply, please send your resume to dhsnext@dhs.gov.

A mission like no other

For nearly 15 years, across three administrations, our mission has always been the same: to safeguard our homeland. Every day, we:

Screen 2 million airline passengers and 1 million pieces of luggage Transportation Security Administration
Welcome 2,000 new citizens into our country Citizenship and Immigration Services
Process 282,000 privately owned vehicles and 72,000 truck, rail, and sea containers at ports of entry Customs and Border Protection
Save 10 lives in more than 45 search and rescue operations Coast Guard
Obtain 5 convictions for human smuggling Immigration and Customs Enforcement
Block 1,900 possible cyber intrusions Cyber
Protect 1.4 million federal employees and visitors at 9,000 facilities across the country Federal Protective Service
Prevent circulation of $160,000 in counterfeit currency Secret Service
Provide $17.6 million in emergency management assistance to state, local, and tribal governments Federal Emergency Management Agency
