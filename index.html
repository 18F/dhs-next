Available positions

Cloud engineer

You will be responsible for deploying and scaling various services, troubleshooting issues, and ensuring the general availability and robustness of our cloud infrastructure. Also, you will explore platform as a service capabilities to provide rapid and easy deployment solutions. Multiple positions available.

Senior agile coach

You'll be responsible for bringing lean and agile thinking to various organizations within DHS. You'll help foster environments of sucessful deliver and continuous improvement. You'll have to be effective at convincing indviduals and groups about the value of agile. You'll also work at the enterprise level to re-design many processes to be agile friendly. Multiple positions available.

Technology analyst

You'll spend lots of time learning about, and understanding the value new and exciting cutting edge technologies can bring to the DHS mission. You'll help set up a team that mission programs can come to, to get interesting solutions to exciting problems. You'll also liase and work with the best and brightest technology companies and innovators. Multiple positions available.

How to apply

You'll partner with talented civil servants to spread lean startup, human centered design, data science, and agile practices throughout the Department of Homeland Security. Come fundamentally transform how 240,000 people leverage digital technologies to meet the mission of securing our homeland.

It'll be one of the most challenging, yet enriching experiences of your professional life.

To apply, please send a resume and cover letter to Alisa Mathis (alisa.mathis@hq.dhs.gov).
